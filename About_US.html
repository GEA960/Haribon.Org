    General Info. about Haribon Aerial Delivery Company

    This company’s business named as ‘HARIBON’ is derived from the Philippine Eagle. ‘HARIBON’ it is all about aerial delivery in which the company can deliver products through aircrafts or drones. The pioneers have chosen the Aerial services to work with because Philippines suffers from heavy traffic that affecting almost industries leading into a major recession of the economy. It has low energy consumption given that all services are made with marginal sophistication.

    It is an easy service for most of the people and company. The researchers make a company and name it HARIBON. It is a company that can serve Filipinos in faster and easy way. This aerial delivery is unique for it will be the first in its kind here in the Philippines because it involves aviation that is very rare in the country. The drone it is much inexpensive than an airplane. It is better than land transporting vehicles because it is faster and yet a safe way of transporting different goods and parcels.

    If given the chance and become successful, aerial delivery here in the Philippines will definitely be the mainstream in terms of cargo delivery.

    Once the company that wishes its success became progressive in the span of 5 years, board members will begin their experimentation stage of its new concept, the mapping and rescue operations.

    Our Mission

    Our mission is to provide quality service with the combination of punctuality and cargo safety. And to have a sustainable and profitable income for the company and to its employees. And also to be a rewarding and cozy place for employees and its stakeholders. Lastly. to have a clear and feasible progression in the future. Our mission will not end there, because our company will continue to strive to help the community by bringing superior and dependable service. To be recognized in the whole country of the Philippines as the first successful and prolific aerial delivery company. And with the help of each other, stable and marginal operation is always observed with high awareness. Leading to a more productive and progressive future.
